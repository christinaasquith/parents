"Quaranteam 2020"

Our best efforts here to create a school year that provides:

  • Kids learn & stay connected to CVSD
  • Play socially
  • Parents have reliable child care

Proposal

3 or so families and 6-ish kids "quaranteam" and provide a mini-school from 9-5 each school day.

We hire a full-time teacher to run this and all pay in to cover.

Learning is project-based and goal-driven, not curriculum-driven

Each parent contribute one, two-hour activity a week

Orientation is to encourage scientific-mindset, self-motivation and curiosity

We rotate among the houses.

Field Trips are Great!
